Our Key Initiatives

Providing Daily Meals

We ensure that street dogs have access to nutritious meals every day.

Veterinary Care & Vaccination

Providing essential veterinary care, vaccinations, and medical treatment to keep dogs healthy.

Shelter & Safe Havens

Creating safe shelters and adoption programs to give dogs a loving home.

Why Your Support Matters

Improved Health & Well-being

Your contribution ensures that street dogs receive the nutrition and care they need to live healthy lives.

Reduced Animal Suffering

By providing shelter and medical care, we alleviate the suffering of vulnerable animals living on the streets.

Building a Compassionate Community

Together, we can create a community that values and protects the welfare of all animals.

Long-Term Impact

Your support helps us implement sustainable programs that create lasting positive change for street dogs.

About Feedmydog

We are a dedicated team of animal lovers committed to improving the lives of street dogs. Our mission is to provide food, medical care, and shelter to these vulnerable animals, and to promote compassion and awareness within our community.
